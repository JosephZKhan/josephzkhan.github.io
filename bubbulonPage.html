Enter Planet Bubbulon
Jan 2025

Enter Planet Bubbulon is a 2D score-attack game I developed solo in Unity for the 2025 Global Game Jam, where the given theme was "Bubbles". Brainstorming unique ways to use bubbles lead to the idea of mining a bubble-planet, which naturally came with a form of danger in bursting the very planet you stand on.

I created the pixel art assets in Aseprite. I limited myself to the ZX Spectrum colour palette, going for a style similar to Jetpac.
