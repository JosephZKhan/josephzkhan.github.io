A demonstration for a controllable camera that can be used in a 3D platform game. In addition to the basic functionality of following the player, this camera can snap to 90-degree angles around the player as well as smoothly move behind the player when they lock on to an object. The camera can also enter a first-person view that can be zoomed in and out.
