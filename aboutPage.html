Hi there! My name is Joseph.

I'm an aspiring game developer, looking for a place in the industry.
I have always had a deep admiration for the medium of gaming, and I feel its level of interactivity can deliver an unparallelled experience to the player.
I'm currently studying Games Technology at the University of the West of England (UWE), a TIGA-accredited games development course.
I like to dabble in many facets of the game development process. While I consider myself a designer at heart, I have been working on my programming skills, with experience using Python, C++, and HLSL.
I have also found a creative outlet in pixel art. I developed the hobby during the first national lockdown in 2020, and built an Instagram account dedicated to pixel-art recreations of famous and iconic album covers.
When I'm not working on video games, I play the alto saxophone and guitar, and enjoy going on long hikes through natural scenery.
