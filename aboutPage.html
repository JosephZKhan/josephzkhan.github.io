About Me

Hello! I'm Joseph.


I'm a software developer with 3 years' commercial development experience.

I am proficient in a range of languages and technologies, as a result of my experiences across multiple studios and my First-Class BSc in Games Technology.

I have worked professionally in C# and TypeScript (respectively, 2 years and 8 months), and have otherwise made projects in C++, GDScript, HTML and Python.

I am confident developing with both Unity and Unreal game engines, having 2 years' professional experience in the former.

I always strive to maintain clear yet concise communication with fellow developers, as I credit much of a production's success to the communication skills used to handle complex issues.

I am based in Bristol, UK, but open to relocation for the right role.

When I'm not working on or otherwise playing video games, I play the alto saxophone and guitar, and enjoy bouldering and hiking.
