Hello! I'm Joseph.


I'm an aspiring game developer, looking for a place in the industry.

I've had a lifelong appreciation for the gaming medium, and always appreciate games with deep and engaging design to their mechanics and levels.

I have recently graduated with a First-Class Honours from the University of the West of England (UWE), from their Games Technology BSc course.

I am honoured to have been nominated two UK Games Education awards by The Independent Game Developers' Association (TIGA): Graduate of the Year in both the Games Technology and Programming categories.

On an extended internship, I had the pleasure to spend two years working with Ground Shatter, a BAFTA-nominated indie studio in Bristol, gaining hands-on development experience programming on Knights In Tight Spaces, a roguelike deckbuilder available now on Steam.

I am confident in programming and design within Unity and Unreal, having used both for numerous solo and group projects. Within Unity, I have extensive practice scripting custom editors for specific properties and classes. I have also scripted within GDScript for Godot.

When I'm not working on or otherwise playing video games, I play the alto saxophone and guitar, and enjoy bouldering and hiking.
