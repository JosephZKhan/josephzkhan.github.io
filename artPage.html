  • CV

I have been using Aseprite since 2018, a hobby that has since lead to my involvement in a range of projects, from designing Spotify profile pictures to clothing deals. I have also applied this to game dev, creating and animating assets for 2D projects.

8 Bit Album Covers

Outside of video games, one of my greatest passions lies in music. I make an effort to be well-listened in a variety of genres, and have always been drawn towards the imagery attached to certain artists and albums. As a way to occupy myself during the early lockdowns in 2020, I decided to bring my passions for pixel art and music together, and started an Instagram account dedicated to remaking classic or iconic albums in pixel art. I've run this account ever since, and it has grown to over 4,200 followers. You can find my account here, and some of my best work is featured below.

Other Work

Beyond this page, I have made portraits in pixel-art, and contributed 2D asset art and animation for the game Wardens Teddy. For more information about this game, see here.
