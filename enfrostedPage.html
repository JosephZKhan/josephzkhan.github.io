Enfrosted
Sep 2024 - May 2025

Enfrosted is 3D platformer where you play as a snowman, gaining special powers for traversal and exploration as you encounter other snowmen with magic scarves along your journey.

I was joint Project Lead, and the main Design Lead. This involved concieving the game's core mechanics and progression structure, along with leading the construction of level scenes and environments.

A demo is available now on itch.io.

It is made in Unreal Engine 5, using FMOD for additional audio operations. Git has been used for source control. Trello, Github Wiki and Google Sheets are used as management software.
