Unity

Reaction-Diffusion Effect

This project was an attempt to recreate the swirling blood effect found on the death screen of Control (2019). After learning about Reaction-Diffusion, the mathematical model used to represent differently-coloured chemicals diffusing into one another, I found a way to implement this using multiple shader files updating and reading from a custom render target. The resultant effect shows a pattern spreading across a surface. The visible properties and diffusion rates of the two 'chemicals' can be customized, and the effect works on any mesh, allowing for a variety of uses.
