I have been fortunate enough to have gained development experience through participation in several game jams. In 2020, I formed a small indie studio with my friends, Top Lad Productions. Our first project, Wardens Teddy, was released for free on itch.io in 2021.

Titles

Wardens Teddy

A 5D puzzle-platformer, initially built for the 2021 Global Game Jam and later expanded into a complete experience and released on itch.io. I contributed to the level design, and was responsible for all asset art/animation. Made in Unity.

Orbital Detachment

An observation game where you view creatures on an alien planet from an orbiting spaceship. Built in 48 hours for the 2019 FuseJam. My contributions included game/character design, writing, 2D art and foley work. Made in Unity.
