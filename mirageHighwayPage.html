Mirage Highway
Sep 2025 - Ongoing

An arcade platformer/shoot-em-up in the supernatural Wild West, where grizzled cowboys navigate treacherous terrain, blasting foes on all sides. Who can find the fabled Mirage City at the end of the highway?

This is a solo venture, inspired by titles including several games in the UFO 50 multipack (most notably Ninpek and Campanella 3), along with Downwell and the animal-riding levels in Crash Bandicoot. It's currently just a prototype, but development is enthusiastically ongoing!

Influenced by the elegance of simplicity in UFO 50, the player's movement is restricted to 5 lanes to maintain focus on key points of action.

The multiple directions of shooting (sideways, up and forwards) and the combination of platforming and shoot-em-up gameplay are standout features that I aim to develop through production.

I hope to take this further with procedurally generated levels to extend its longevity, and having accrued points/distance over multiple runs unlock content including playable characters.

As I expand the variety of enemies and develop environmental art, I aim to expand on the theming by adding a paranormal twist to the Wild West setting. Building on the idea of mirages, I'm aiming to introduce mirage-based ghosts, which could lead to some interesting shader development.

This was made in Unreal Engine 5.6, programmed in C++.

All pixel art was drawn in Aseprite, and all audio in Furnace.
